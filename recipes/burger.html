Burger

Description

This hamburger recipe is flavorful, juicy, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings, herbs, and sauces
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a bowl
  2. Make four patties
  3. Grill patties until juices run clear and meat is slightly pink in center
